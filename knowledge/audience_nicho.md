## Niche Selection Framework

### Good Niche Criteria
1. **Passionate audience**: They actively seek solutions
2. **Painful problem**: Strong desire to solve it
3. **Willingness to pay**: Proven market
4. **Your expertise**: You can teach it
5. **Not too saturated**: Room for differentiation
6. **Sustainable**: Long-term content potential

## Positioning Strategy

### The 3 Positioning Types

#### 1. Technique Expert
```
"I teach THE BEST [specific technique]"
Examples:
- "The Nmap Masterclass"
- "Mastering SQL Injection"
- "Windows Privilege Escalation Secrets"

USP: You have unique knowledge
```

#### 2. Results Coach
```
"I help you achieve [specific result]"
Examples:
- "From IT Admin to Pentester in 90 Days"
- "Your First $10K Month as a Bug Bounty Hunter"
- "Pass the OSCP on Your First Try"

USP: You guarantee outcomes
```

#### 3. Systems Builder
```
"I give you THE COMPLETE SYSTEM"
Examples:
- "The Complete Cybersecurity Career System"
- "The Bug Bounty Blueprint"
- "The Ethical Hacking Framework"

USP: Comprehensive solution
```

## Content Strategy by Platform

### YouTube
```
PRIMARY GOAL: Awareness + Authority
AUDIENCE: Beginners to intermediate
FORMAT: Long tutorials, walkthroughs
FREQUENCY: 1-2 videos per week
MONETIZATION: Courses, affiliate
```

### Twitter/X
```
PRIMARY GOAL: Thought leadership
AUDIENCE: Peers, potential buyers
FORMAT: Threads, tips, opinions
FREQUENCY: 3-5 tweets daily
MONETIZATION: Consulting, courses
```

### LinkedIn
```
PRIMARY GOAL: Professional authority
AUDIENCE: Decision makers, HR
FORMAT: Articles, case studies
FREQUENCY: 3-5 posts per week
MONETIZATION: Premium courses, consulting
```

### TikTok/Shorts
```
PRIMARY GOAL: Viral reach, younger audience
AUDIENCE: Gen Z, beginners
FORMAT: Quick tips, hacks, reactions
FREQUENCY: Daily
MONETIZATION: Traffic to main platform
```

## Retention Strategies

### For Students
1. **Quick wins**: First lesson = immediate value
2. **Progress tracking**: Badges, certificates
3. **Community access**: Peer connection
4. **Instructor presence**: Regular check-ins
5. **Updates**: Keep content fresh

### For Email List
1. **Value-first**: 80% tips, 20% sales
2. **Personal voice**: Not corporate
3. **Segmentation**: Send relevant content
4. **Consistency**: Same schedule
5. **Exclusivity**: Email-only perks
